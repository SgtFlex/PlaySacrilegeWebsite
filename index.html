"Sacrilege", a Halo fan-game, is an Open-source, free shooter that aims to offer a wide variety of gamemodes, weapons, vehicles, enemies, and more.


Bountiful allies, and enemies

Sacrilege aims to offer a diverse cast of enemies and allies that you will be able to fight, and ally with. You can expect to fight enemies with unique behaviors, and enemies that think for themselves and make decisions in the heat of combat.

A Powerful Armory

Sacrilege features a sleuth of options when it comes to weapons. You can expect to find classic weapons such as the Covenant Carbine, Fuel Rod Cannon, Battle Rifle, and much more, and new weapons, such as the Forerunner Gravity Manipulator

Vehicle Mayhem

The game features a wide variety of vehicle options, such as Warthogs, Ghosts, Scorpions, Spectres, Mongooses, and more. More are planned, as Vehicles are a core tennent of Sacrilege.


Details for the Microsoft Game Content Usage Rules (GCUR) can be found here: https://www.xbox.com/en-US/developers/rules


All present weapon models were created by various artists credited on the main menu under "Attributions".

"Sacrilege", a Halo fan-game, is an Open-source, free shooter that aims to offer a wide variety of gamemodes, weapons, vehicles, enemies, and more.

You are the commander

Bountiful allies, and enemies

Fight a diverse cast of foes each with unique behaviors, such as the Covenant fodder, the Grunts, or the Elites, enemies capable of aggresive and intelligent positioning, or fight the Flood, who are capable of turning anyone against them.

Furthermore, these diverse cast of foes can also be called down as allies.

A Powerful Armory

Fight your foes with classic UNSC weapons such as the Assault Rifle, Battle Rifle, Sniper Rifle, and more, or defeat them and wield covenant weapons such as the Carbine, Needler, Plasma Rifle, and more. Perhaps you will find remnants of Forerunner technology, such as the Forerunner Gravity Manipulator, capable of picking up objects and placing them or launching them.

Vehicle Mayhem

Drive a wide variety of vehicle options, such as Warthogs, Ghosts, Scorpions, Spectres, Mongooses, and more. More are planned, as Vehicles are a core tennent of Sacrilege.

Playful Sandbox

Use your map knowledge to destroy tactfully placed explosive barrels, use a combination of Barbed barricades and the Forerunner Gravity Gun to make blockades, or use your wits to create a ridiculous Rude Goldberg machine by spawning a variety of objects, such as Gravity Lifts, Explosive Barrels, Vehicles, and much more.
